Kyoto, Japan is renowned for its beautiful temples, traditional tea houses, and stunning gardens. Take a stroll through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, visit the iconic Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and experience the serene atmosphere of the city’s historic districts.

New Zealand offers breathtaking landscapes from mountains to beaches. Enjoy activities like bungee jumping in Queenstown, exploring the geothermal wonders of Rotorua, and hiking the famous Tongariro Alpine Crossing.

Istanbul, Turkey is a city that bridges Europe and Asia, rich in history and culture. Explore the magnificent Hagia Sophia, the bustling Grand Bazaar, and the stunning Blue Mosque, while enjoying delicious Turkish cuisine at local restaurants.
